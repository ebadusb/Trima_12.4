Focussed GUI Prototype

Naming conventions for common buttons:


BTN_ABCD.bmp
    ||||
    |||+ d=down image; u=up image
    ||+ color: g=green; b=blue
    |+ screen location: t=top; b=bottom; m=mid-screen
    + size: s=small; b=big; d=big data button 


Example:

   BTN_STGU.BMP - this is a small, green button on the status bar in the
                   up position.
